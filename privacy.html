TRT Med Reminders Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 7, 2026

Overview

TRT Med Reminders is a medication reminder app. This policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

We collect your email address when you create an account. The app may also store medication reminder information you enter, such as medication names, dose details, schedules, reminder status, and reminder history, so the app can provide its core features.

How We Use Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To provide medication reminder, schedule, and history features.
  • To respond to support requests.
  • To maintain app reliability and security.

No Sale of Personal Information

We do not sell your personal information or app data to anyone.

Sharing Information

We do not share your personal information with advertisers or data brokers. We may use service providers that help operate the app, host the website, process account data, or provide technical infrastructure. Those providers are used only to support the app and website.

Medical Disclaimer

TRT Med Reminders is not medical advice and does not replace advice from a licensed clinician. Always follow instructions from your healthcare provider.

Your Choices

You may contact us to request account deletion or ask privacy questions. When possible, we will delete or update account information associated with your email address.

Children's Privacy

TRT Med Reminders is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will update the date at the top of this page.

Contact

For privacy questions or account deletion requests, contact support.
